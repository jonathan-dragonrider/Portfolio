About Me

Hello, my name is Jon Kline. I am 22 years old, and live in Indiana. I was raised in Evansville, and went to West Lafayette in 2016 to attend Purdue University College of Engineering. I graduated in spring 2020 with a B.S. in Materials Science and Engineering with a 3.2 out of 4.0 GPA. Alongside working for my degree, I was involved in competitive robotics, undergraduate research, technical conferences, and peer teaching. I did a Maymester in China, a technology internship with SABIC, and an electronics reliability study for NSWC Crane as my senior design project. After graduation, I spent two months doing business consulting for GarageExperts in Evansville while trying to find an engineering job. After realizing that programming is my true passion, I decided to enroll in a full-time software development bootcamp through Eleven Fifty Academy, which is my current pursuit. I hope to transition into a career in software development.
